San Francisco: the best and the worst.

The Haight

The Haight is my favorite neighborhood because one of my favorite restaurants is on this street and I love the vibes from the people and the design elements.

Financial District

Financial District is my least favorite simply because it is so overwhelmingly busy that I stay away unless I absolutely have to go. It seems like a good idea at first but once you get there you wish you would have just stayed home and ordered in. :P
